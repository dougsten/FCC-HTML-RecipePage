BLT Sandwich

This is how to make a BLT Sandwich and the ingredients you will need.

Ingredients

  • Bacon (two slices cooked)
  • Lettuce (two leaves)
  • Tomato (1 small to medium, sliced)
  • Bread (two slices)

Instructions

  1. Place one slice of Bread on preparation surface.
  2. Place Bacon on Bread evenly istributed.
  3. Place Tomato on top of Bacon.
  4. Place Lettuce on top of Tomato and top with 2nd slice of Bread
